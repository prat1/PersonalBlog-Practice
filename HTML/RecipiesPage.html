HyderbadBiryani

Posted On: Nov,2020

HyderbadBiryani is a delicious food with lots of spices and aromatic food with rice and chicken.
It is the authuentic and traditional age old recipie which is loved all over the world. Strong aroma from the spices, tender chicken, steamed rice makes it mouth watering.
Eventhough, the ingredients list is long, its prepareation is easy and simple to cook if follwed with the reciepe.

Ingredients list
Items Quantity
Basmati Rice 2 cups
Chicken 1 kgs
Yogurt 1 cup
Ginger-Garlic Paste 4 tbsp
Whole Spices each 3 pieces
Garam Masala 4 tbps
Onions chopped 3 medium
Corriender and Mint leaves A handfull each
Biriyani masala Powder 1 packet
Ghee 5 tbps
Green chillies 5 chopped
Red chilli powder 5 tbps
Tumeric Powder 2 tbps
Salt Acoording to taste

First chop the chicken in to big size pieces and clean them nicely. Add salt according to taste and 2 tbps of tumeric powder to it. Also add ginger garlic paste, yogurt,garam masala, some corriender and mint leaves, biriyani masala powder, ghee, green chillies, red chilli powder to it. Now mix all the ingredients well and massage the chicken nicely. In another pan add oil and deep fry onions untill golden brown and then add the onions to the chicken marinate.

Now take the basmati rice and soak it in warm water for 30 minutes and add salt and whole spieces to it and boil and cook the rice till 75 percent. Strain the water out and take a big low pan and add some oil to it and add whole spieces to it and stir them until brown. Add chicken and add the half cooked rice on top and fix a lit on top and let it cook for 45 minutes

This process locks all the aroma and flavours in the chicken and rice and lets it cook tender. Open the lid and add some corriender and mint leaves and fried onions as topping and serve hot.

Gulab Jamun

Posted On: Oct,2020

Gulab Jamun is the delicious desset made from the goodness of milk powder and milk maid. It is a very common dessert in India and it is rich, soft, creamy and delicate.
Easy to make, but precision is important. Little patience and the amazing sweet is yours to enjoy. Very few ingredients, little patience and the dessert is ready. Lets cook up!!

Ingredients list
Items Quantity
Milk Powder 2 cups
All Purpose Flour 1 cup
Milk Maid 1 tin
Oil 1 liter
Sugar 600 grams
Water 1 liter
Cardamum Powder 2 tbps
Almonds chopped For garnish

Take a flat pan, put it on low flame and put some milk powder in it and keep Stiring. Add the all purpose flour to it and then a pinch of salt. Then add the milk maid and keep cooking the mixture untill a thick dough is formed.

Let the dough cool down and then kneed the dough. Add 2 tbps of ghee and make tiny balls by pressing the dough firmly between the palms. Make sure the balls are soft with no cracks on top.

Now in an another pan heat up the oil and put the balls in oil and fry them till golden brown. Keep them aside.

Now in an another pan bring the water to boil add the sugar and cardmum powder to it and let the sugar dissolve and boil the solution till it forms string like sticky consistency.Now lower the flame and add the balls to it and let them soak in the syrup for atleast 2 hrs.

Now take them in a serving bowl and add some chopped almonds and serve either hot or cold. When hot, it can be eatten with vanilla ice cream.
